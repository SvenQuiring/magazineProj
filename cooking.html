Carrot Cake

Ingredients:



  • 350 grams of grated carrot.
  • 250 grams of wheat flour.
  • 4 eggs.
  • 120 grams of normal sugar.
  • 150 grams of brown sugar.
  • 250 ml of soft olive oil.
  • Teaspoon of cinnamon.
  • A pinch of salt.
  • 2 tablespoons of yeast.
  • For coverage.
  • 800 grams of philadelphia cheese.
  • 140 grams of icing sugar.
    •


Steps:



  1. The first thing we will do is mix the flour, yeast, salt and spices on the one hand.
  2. In another bowl, mix the eggs well with the two types of sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Add the flour mixture little by little until a homogeneous mixture remains.
  4. Next we add the carrots. Previously we will have peeled and finely grated, we must have 350 grams of grated carrots.
  5. The oil will be the last, we add it in the form of thread little by little until all the ingredients are well integrated.
  6. We grease an oven mold about 20 cm in diameter. We incorporate the mixture and bake it at 180º for about 45 minutes. To know if it is ready, click with a toothpick and when it comes out dry, it will be ready
  7. We remove and let cool while we make the coverage.
  8. In a bowl, mix the cream cheese atroom temperature with the icing sugar until everything is integrated.
  9. When the cake is cold, we break it in half as you see in the image and fill with the coverage. We can really make 3 or 4 layers but I did not want to make two biscuits so that it came out high,so it seemed to me that it was fine and I assure you that it will take less and it will be just as delicious.
  10. Cover the cake with all the cheese topping and put it in the fridge to cool down.


Brownie

Ingredients:



  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablespoons of salted butter melted.
  • 1 cup granulated sugar.
  • 2 large eggs.
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.
  • 1/2 cup melted milk chocolate chips.
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour.
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips.
    •


Steps:



  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line a metal 9x9 pan with parchment paper.
  2. Pour melted butter into a large mixing bowl. Whisk in sugar by hand untilsmooth, 30 seconds.
  3. Add in eggs and vanilla extract. Whisk 1 minute.
  4. Whisk in melted chocolate until combined and smooth.
  5. Use a rubber spatula to stirin flour, cocoa powder, and salt until just combined. Stirin whole chocolate chips.
  6. Pour into prepared pan and smooth out.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Let cool in pan 30 minutes before slicing.


Banana Bread

Ingredients:



  • 2 cups flour.
  • 1 cup sugar.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla.
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon.
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder.
  • 1/2 cup butter, margarine, or vegetable oil.
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/4 cup milk.
  • 3 regular size bananas.
    •





Steps:



  1. Well Ripe bananas should be used for more flavor in the bread.
  2. We put the bananas in a very clean and dry container and crush until making a consistent puree.
  3. Then we mix in another container the two eggs, sugar, oil, milk and vanilla. Then we add the rest of the dry ingredients and little by little the mashed bananas.
  4. For better consistency, integrate everything with enveloping movements so that it is spongy.
  5. Grease the mold with butter or margarine and turn the oven on at 220 degrees c. For 10 minutes. While we pour the mixture into the container and tap a little to remove the bubbles from the mixture.
  6. Ready!

Pasta Frola

Ingredients:



  • 2 cups flour.
  • ½ cups sugar.
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder.
  • 1 pinch of salt.
  • 100gr of butter.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 2 tablespoons of milk.
  • ½ teaspoon of vanilla.
  • Lemon zest.
  • 400gr of quince or dulce de leche.
  • 1 egg yolk.
    •


Steps:



  1. Place the two cups of sifted flour, the sugar, the baking powder and the salt in a bowl..
  2. To the previous mixture add the butter at room temperature, and undo it until it is well mixed.
  3. In a cup mix the eggs, milk, vanilla, and lemon, beat lightly and add to the bowl with the dry mixture.
  4. Knead well and separate ¾ of the dough.
  5. Roll out 3/4 of the dough and place in a buttered pan.
  6. Crush the quince with a splash of hot water and put on top of the stretched dough.
  7. Roll out the remaining dough and cut 2cm straight lines, place it above the quince.
  8. You can paint the edges with the yolk of the egg, and finally take to a preheated oven at 180°.


Cinnamon-Roll Pancakes

Ingredients:



    CINNAMON FILLING:

  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened.
  • ⅓ cup light brown sugar.
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour.
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon.
  • Pinch of salt.

    • PANCAKES:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder.
  • Pinch of salt.
  • 1½ cups buttermilk.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted.
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract.
  • Nonstick cooking spray, as needed.

    • ICING:

  • 1½ cups confectioners’ sugar.
  • 3 tablespoons whole milk.
  • ½ teaspoon pure vanilla extract.
    •


Steps:



    MAKE THE CINNAMON FILLING:

  1. In a medium bowl, mix the butter with the brown sugar, flour, cinnamon and salt to combine.
  2. Transfer to a medium plastic zip-top bag.
  3. Squeeze the filling down into one corner of the bag and snip the corner of the bag with scissors.
  4. Set aside.

    MAKE THE PANCAKES

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the flour with the baking powder and salt to combine.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk the buttermilk with the eggs, melted butter and vanilla extract to combine.
  3. Add the buttermilk mixture to the flour mixture and whisk until smooth.
  4. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium heat. Spray the hot pan with nonstick cooking spray.
  5. Working in batches of 3, ladle or scoop ⅓ cup of pancake batter onto a large skillet or griddle. Cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Squeeze a spiral of the cinnamon filling on top of the pancakes. Continue to cook 1 to 2 minutes..
  6. Flip the pancakes and cook for 2 to 3 minutes on the other side. Remove from the pan and continue with the remaining batter and cinnamon filling. Set aside.

    7. MAKE THE ICING:

  7. Whisk the confectioners’ sugar with the milk and vanilla extract to combine.
  8. Add additional milk in small amounts, as needed, to make the icing easy to drizzle.
  9. To serve, stack three pancakes on each plate and either drizzle with the icing or serve it on the side.

Avocados with bacon and egg

Ingredients:



  • 4 avocados.
  • 8 small eggs.
  • 150g bacon.
    •








Steps:



  1. Chop the bacon and cook it in a pan, without any fat, until it is golden and crisp. We take it out and leave it on kitchen paper, so that it absorbs all the fat..
  2. We split the avocados in half, remove the bone and with the help of a spoon remove a little avocado, not much, but just enough to fit the egg.
  3. We put a little bacon in the hole and on top of the egg. We put in the microwave at minimum power about two minutes, until the egg is curdled but leaving the yolk liquid.
  4. Take out and serve with the rest of the bacon on top. Cooking an egg in the microwave is complicated, since you have to catch the trick to the power, to get the egg to our liking.

You can also prepare this recipe in the oven. You preheat the oven to 180º C and put the avocados with the egg inside and the bacon on top, in this case you do not need to cook the bacon before, since it is cooked in the oven. If you like the very liquid yolk, you can cook the avocado with the bacon and the white first, and when everything is cooked, put the yolks on top and bake one or two more minutes.

Bourbon BBQ Glazed Pork Chops

Ingredients:



  • 1 tbsp. olive oil.
  • 2 cloves garlic, pressed.
  • 1 tbsp. chili powder.
  • 1/4 tsp. cayenne pepper.
  • 1 c. ketchup.
  • 3/4 c. bourbon (like Jack Daniel’s).
  • 1/4 c. molasses.
  • 1/4 c. cider vinegar.
  • 2 tbsp. dark brown sugar.
  • 2 tbsp. Dijon mustard.
  • 1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce.
  • Kosher salt.
  • Freshly ground black pepper.
  • 8 1-inch-thick bone-in pork chops (about 3 pounds total).
    •










Steps:



  1. Heat oil and garlic in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until garlic sizzles (do not let brown), about 1 minute. Add chili powder and cayenne. Cook, stirring constantly, 1 minute.
  2. Add ketchup, bourbon, molasses, vinegar, sugar, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened, 12 to 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat grill to medium-high. Season chops with salt and pepper. Grill, turning once, until a thermometer inserted in the thickest part (avoiding the bone) registers 135°F, 10 to 14 minutes, basting with glaze (about 2/3 cup) during the last 4 minutes of cooking. Transfer to a platter and baste again. Serve with remaining glaze.
