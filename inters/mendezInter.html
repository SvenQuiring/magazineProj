Marcos Mendez


1-How are you coping with the online classes?
Great. There was an important learning curve, especially with the digitalization and explanation of the new and different subjects..

2-Was it difficult or not?
As a start, yes. But the knowledge will stay forever

3-What was the most difficult part of teaching online?
The interaction. There was no interaction with the student. It was pretty hard for one to say “I didn’t understand something.” and to then try and explain it to him.

4-How did you get used to computer teaching?
Yes, yes because I already had experience with them.

5-How did students react?
There were a lot more insecurities in the beginning, but everyone got used to it till it finally reached a point where the development of a class was comfortable.

6-How do you feel about your students?
Luckily, it surpassed any expectative that I had.

7-Do you fell like you could teach as you planned ?
It took some time, but at the end, yes.
