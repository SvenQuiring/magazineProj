Technology:



In his right hand, specifically between the thumb and index finger, the Swedish Patric Lanhed has amicroprocessor the size of a grain of rice installed. It is a glass capsule made of borosilicate glass, inside which there is a plate with a capacity of 2 kilobytes to store information. For him, it is no longer a necessity to look for the keys to enter his house, or carry a ‘ticket’ when he needs to get on the train. Now you just have to put your hand to an electronic reader to access these places.
