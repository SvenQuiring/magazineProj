GRANDPARENTS GYM:


DOING GYMNASTICS IS HEALTHY AT ANY AGE,BUT IN OLDER PEOPLE IT IS ESSENTIAL TO A VOID THE LOSS OF MUSCLE MASS.


SOME OF THE THINGS WERE COMMEND TO YOU ARE:


WALK IN YOUR GARDEN,OR ON A TREADMILL IF YOU HAVE,FOR ABOUT 45 MINUTES 3 DAYS A WEEK.


HAVE A YOGA CLASS VIA YOUTUBE OR SOME YOGA WEBSITE,AT LEAST 2 DAYS A WEEK.


EXERCISE IN A POOL,OR OUTSIDE THE POOL,(IF YOU HAVE ONE),SINCE THEY HELP STRETCH THE MUSCLES AT LOW INTENSITY.(EXAMPLES:RAISE YOUR ARMS AND LOWER BY STRETCHING THEM,STRETCH YOUR ARMS FORWARD AND PUT YOUR HEAD FACE DOWN ON THE FLOOR)


HAVE A CLASS VIA YOUTUBE OF ZUMBA,AEROBICS,ETC.


IF YOU HAVE A STATIONARY BIKE,YOU CAN USE IT INSTEAD OF WALKING.
